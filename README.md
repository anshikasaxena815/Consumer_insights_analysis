🛍️ Consumer Behavior Analysis in Retail

«Leveraging consumer shopping data to uncover purchasing trends, customer preferences, and actionable business insights using Python, MySQL, and Power BI.»

---

📖 Overview

This project focuses on analyzing consumer shopping behavior in a retail environment to understand purchasing patterns, customer preferences, and loyalty trends.

Using Python for data analysis, MySQL for database querying, and Power BI for visualization, this project follows a complete Data Analytics workflow from data preparation to business reporting.

The project was developed as part of my learning journey in Data Analytics and demonstrates practical application of analytical techniques to solve real-world business problems.

🎯 Business Problem

A leading retail company wants to better understand customer shopping behavior to improve:

- Sales performance
- Customer satisfaction
- Customer retention and loyalty
- Marketing effectiveness
- Product strategy

The objective is to identify the key factors that influence customer purchasing decisions and generate actionable business recommendations.

---

📊 Dataset

The dataset contains retail customer shopping information, including:

- Customer demographics
- Product categories
- Purchase history
- Discounts and promotions
- Customer ratings and reviews
